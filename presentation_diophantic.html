Development process

  1. Idea
  2. Prototype
  3. Pilot
  4. Product

Idea

An idea can lead to either an innovation vacation or a project in the petting zoo.

Prototype

Something which normally can be built in one to two weeks. Something you can demo to potential investors. A prototype should either succeed- or fail fast!

Pilot

A pilot is born out of interest from investors, and should focus on releasing fast and often. The pilot is given to the investors and they run with it, feeding you with feedback and practical ideas.

Product

A product is something which can be shipped. Something with minimally acceptable value but with the potential to satisfy every need.

Return on attention

A new metric to measure innovation

Getting people interested in your product time and again will result in motivated people who will be willing to run with it and invest in it.

Fail fast

Not failing a project but continuing with a stale development task will drain resources, kill motivation and result in sub-par products. Failing fast and killing the failure is much more practical.

Return of investment

You can measure revenue is multiple ways. But taking a purely financial look at the costs of having an idea, building a prototype and starting a pilot will never result in financial gain. You need another way to measure success: Return on attention. Your return on investment will come with the final product.

Being successful

There is more to being a successful business than making a huge profit. Different stakeholders will have different views on what the business must do to be successful.

Strategy

  • Job creation
  • Ethical consideration
  • Customer satisfaction
  • Profit
Strategy: Our goals, our ways to measure our success. We want to be able to create jobs. Get talented people to work with us and innovate. We want people to be amazed by what we do. We want our efforts to result in "well being" We want profit to grow and be able to change.

How will we achieve this?

Investors

When people like what we do they will invest in our ideas and get to run with our prototypes.

Education

We need motivated people to help us innovate. We will start at creating a place where people can learn. We will take on students and form them into innovators. We will take on every branch be it Legal, Business or Technical. We groom them and they help us innovate.
